About the Conference

PyCon Angola is the annual Python programming conference for professionals, entrepreneurs, scientists, academics, and students from Angola and the region.

Python's natural habitat expands to Angola. PyCon Angola is born to connect the country's technology community, promoting knowledge exchange and strengthening the digital ecosystem.

Each edition will bring together speakers from Angola, the African continent, and countries around the world, sharing knowledge, building connections, and strengthening the technology community in Angola.

Learn more

PyCon Angola is part of a global series of Python conferences, bringing to the country the best in talks, practical workshops, open-source sprints, and a welcoming community spirit.

What happens at PyCon Angola?

Three days of immersion in the Python ecosystem

Python Day

Dedicated to Python development, exploring the language's ecosystem and its applications.

  • Workshops on fundamentals and advanced topics
  • Talks with Angolan and international professionals
  • Live coding sessions and mentorship

Education Day

Introduces high school and university students to the world of technology and programming.

  • Introduction to Python programming for beginners
  • Hands-on demonstrations and projects
  • Career talks and mentorship sessions

Main Conference

The centerpiece of PyCon Angola. Talks, workshops, and panels with Python experts.

  • Keynote talks with global experts
  • Practical workshops and project labs
  • Networking and mentorship opportunities
