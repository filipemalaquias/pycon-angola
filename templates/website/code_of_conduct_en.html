Code of Conduct

A safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone

Our Mission

PyCon Angola is a conference dedicated to the Python community, created to promote learning, collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all participants, regardless of age, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, ethnicity, nationality, religion, level of experience, or any other personal characteristic.

Expected Behavior

All participants should:

  • Treat all people with respect and professionalism.
  • Encourage a collaborative and inclusive environment.
  • Respect different opinions.
  • Use appropriate language.
  • Help new community members.
  • Respect the event schedule and guidelines.
  • Comply with local laws and event policies.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

Harassment

Of any nature

Discrimination

Any form of prejudice

Insults

Threats or offenses

Intimidation

Threatening behavior

Offensive Comments

About gender, race, religion, disability, or sexual orientation

Physical Contact

Unsolicited or unwanted

Photography

Recording participants against their will

Disruptions

Constant interruptions of talks

Abusive Language

Swearing or offensive language

Aggressive Behavior

Violent attitudes

Illegal Activities

Any action against the law

Harassment

Harassment includes, but is not limited to:

  • Offensive comments.
  • Discriminatory jokes.
  • Verbal or physical intimidation.
  • Stalking.
  • Sharing personal information without authorization.
  • Unwanted sexual attention.
  • Offensive messages on social media related to the event.

Enforcement

This Code of Conduct applies to:

Participants
Organizers
Speakers
Sponsors
Volunteers
Exhibitors
Guests

Including: In-person conference, Workshops, Hackathons, Social events, Online channels, and Official social media.

Reports

If you witness or are a victim of any inappropriate behavior, please notify the organization immediately.

Reports will be handled with:

  • Confidentiality.
  • Impartiality.
  • Without any form of retaliation.

Disciplinary Measures

The organization may:

  • Issue a verbal warning.
  • Issue a written warning.
  • Request that the behavior cease immediately.
  • Remove the participant from a specific activity.
  • Expel the participant from the event without refund.
  • Prevent participation in future PyCon Angola editions.
  • Notify the relevant authorities when necessary.
