PyDay Angola

The first step towards PyCon Angola

What is PyDay Angola?

PyDay Angola is a preparatory event for PyCon Angola, an initiative to warm up the Python community in the country. It will take place in Namibe, a region with great technological potential that will host the first step of the Python community in Angola.

Building the Python Community in Angola — this is our purpose. PyDay is the starting point to connect enthusiasts, developers, students, and companies who want to be part of the Python ecosystem in Angola.
