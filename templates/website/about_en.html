About PyCon Angola

The first Python conference in Angola

What is PyCon Angola?

PyCon Angola is part of a global series of conferences focused on Python programming.

Bringing together people from all backgrounds to learn, collaborate, and drive progress in technology and open-source projects. The conference is a space where Angola's technology community comes together to share knowledge and build the future.

Participants have the opportunity to network, collaborate on projects, and interact with others who are driving technological advancement in Angola. Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply curious about technology, PyCon Angola has something for everyone.

What happens at PyCon Angola?

A diverse program for all experience levels:

Pre-Conference Tutorials

To kickstart your learning before the main event.

Keynote Speakers

Experts from around the world sharing knowledge and experiences.

Workshops

On web development, automation, data science, and more.

Networking Events

Connect with developers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts.

Open Source Sprints

Contributions to real projects and collaboration with the open-source community.

Lightning Talks

Short, focused ideas from the community in a fast-paced format.

Who makes this possible?

Python Angola

PyCon Angola is organized by the Python Angola community, a non-profit organization dedicated to the growth of the Python ecosystem in the country.

The event is made possible by the generosity of sponsors and volunteers, and all revenue is reinvested in the local Python community, supporting educational initiatives, events, and the strengthening of the technology community in Angola.
